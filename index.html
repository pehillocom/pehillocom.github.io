Property

Pehillo is a 500 year old renovated family farmhouse surrounded by 42 acres of pure serenity. Walking through the property, you’ll see sprawling sunflower fields, hundreds of pine trees, poplar trees and wild animals peacefully enjoying their surroundings.

The Vacation Home

Part of the farmhouse has been completely renovated into two separate fully equipped vacation homes each with 3 bedrooms that can hold 6 people (or 8 people including 2 sleeping on the pull out couch) and 2 bathrooms.

The home has been restored to preserve the historic feel of the property while still providing the modern amenities that you need to fully enjoy your vacation. Each rental includes a fully equipped private patio to enjoy meals in the sun and on the property, you’ll also find a large south-facing swimming pool perfect for relaxing with a drink at sunset as you hear birds chirping and watch deer walk by.

The Region

Pehillo is located in the historic royal city of Nerac between Bordeaux and Toulouse, about 80 miles from each and a short drive away from beautiful local villages that will give you a glimpse into the heart of the Southwest of France.

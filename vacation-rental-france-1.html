Vacation Rental 1

The first vacation rental can accommodate up to 6 people and features:

  • Ground floor
    • A fully equipped kitchen and living room with a pull out couch
    • 2 separate bedrooms with a large double bed in each (180 cm) that can be converted into two 90 cm twin beds if needed
    • A bathroom with a bathtub/shower and sink (to be shared between the 2 bedrooms)
    • A separate room with a toilet
  • Upstairs
    • Bedroom with two 90 cm twin beds that can be converted into a large 180 cm double bed if needed
    • A private separate bathroom with a shower, sink and a toilet
  • A private outdoor area with terrace to enjoy meals outdoors while overlooking the countryside
  • Swimming pool access from May to October
  • WiFi
  • Pets are not allowed


* Renting the full vacation rental during high and very high seasons can only be done for a minimum of 1 week from Saturday to Saturday.

Off-Season Rentals

During the off season, from September to June, you also have the option of only booking 1-2 bedrooms rather than the full 3 bedroom vacation rental at a lower price (this option is not available during holiday weekends).

This option includes:

  • Bed sheets, towels, full cleaning upon departure (or more regularly depending on your preference)
  • 1 bathroom with organic beauty products provided (shampoo, body wash, bar of soap etc.)
  • Private living room with TV and DVD player with access to fridge, coffee machine, boiler and microwave
  • Wifi and business services upon request (printing and scanning)
  • Access to the swimming pool from May to June

Vacation Rental with a pool for 4 guests

Gîte de la Sapinière

Vacation Rental with a pool in Nérac – Lot et Garonne. Le Gîte de la Sapinière is about 800 sqft and is our most private rental available. It is located in a renovated stone-built farmhouse and has a relaxing véranda, a private terrace with garden furniture, wood barbecue, deck chairs and umbrella. A maximum of 4 people can be accommodated in 2 bedrooms and 1 bathroom, all on the same floor.

The vacation rental can accommodate up to 4 people and feature:

  • One bedroom with a king size bed (180 cm)
  • One bedroom with a queen size bed (160 cm) that can be converted into two 80 cm twin beds if requested
  • A bathroom with a bathtub/shower and sink (to be shared between the 2 bedrooms)
  • A separate room with a toilet
Book Now

Each room is equipped with a fan in the summer, central heat in the winter and each bathroom has a hair dryer and towel drying rack. The main room on the first floor includes an open kitchen that is connected to the living room with direct access to the private patio. The private patio has a dining table, dining chairs for 4 people and 2 lounge chairs, an umbrella and a charcoal grill (charcoal not included and should be purchased by renter if desired).

The wifi is included and access to the pool is shared with all occupants of the property (the pool is open from June 15 to September 15 - weather dependent). Pets are not admitted on the property to preserve the peace of the wildlife present.

Our rentals are open year round for stays of 2 nights minimum. They are available only on a weekly basis (from Saturday to Saturday) between July and August.

Equipment

The kitchen is fully equipped for your needs. In it you will find a dishwasher, a large fridge (2 small fridges in one of the rentals), a freezer, a ceramic cooktop with 4 heaters, a classic filter coffee maker and an electric kettle, a toaster, an electric oven, a microwave, silverware and dining ware and a washing machine and a drying rack.

Travel Light

Moving forward, all rentals include bed sheets and bath towels (pool towels not included) and the final cleaning at checkout. The kitchen also includes oil, vinegar, pepper and some spices as well as a clean sponge, liquid dish soap, a garbage bag and a few dishwasher tables for the start of your stay.

Kid Friendly

We welcome children on the property and try our best to make their stay as comfortable as possible by providing in each rental, by request, a baby bed, a high chair, a potty chair and a potty seat.

Community Living

The rental is shared with other renters and requires certain areas to be shared. Therefore, no parties or events are allowed.

Things To Do and See

From visits to local markets and villages, hiking in the countryside and tasting the best local dishes and wines, there are many things to do that will make your stay unforgettable. Here is a sample of what awaits you in Lot-et-Garonne:

Visit Nerac & surrounding villages and cities

About a 5 minute drive from Pehillo, you'll find the charming historic city of Nerac where you can spend time visiting King Henry IV's castle and its museum, trying local cuisine, boating along the Baise river, exploring the traditional Saturday local market and simply strolling up and down the city's beautiful narrow streets.

Pehillo is also located close to many beautiful cities and villages ideal for day trips including Bordeaux, Toulouse, Condom, Fources, Larressingle, Saint-Emilion and Buzet. All of these cities will allow you to explore different parts of the culture and traditions of the Southwest of France.

Wine Country

About an hour and a half drive north of Pehillo, you'll find 5 famous wine trails called the Bordeaux Wine routes. This 55 mile route is filled with beautiful historic castles and vineyards giving you the opportunity to visit one of the world's best known territory for fine wine. You can also visit some of the local vineyards including Buzet, le Brulhois, Duras, le Marmandais, or enjoy a tasting of Armagnac, a distinctive brandy produced in the Armagnac region in Gascony.

Sporting Activities

Want to stay active during your vacation? You can start by exploring the walking trail inside the property to see local wildlife and greenery.In Nerac and the surrounding area, you'll find opportunities to bike, zip line, hike, play tennis and horseback ride.

Amusement Parks

The region's largest amusement parks (Walibi et Aqualand) are about a 30 minute drive from Pehillo - from wave pools to water slides, roller coasters and everything in between, there's no doubt you'll find refreshing fun for the whole family.

Spas & Thermal Baths

The region is known for its thermal baths, a tradition in the Lot-et-Garonne since the Roman times. Many of them offer full spa services that makes for a great day trip to relax and unwind during your vacation.
